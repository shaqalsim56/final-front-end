Welcome To The Admin Dashboard

Manage Vehicles For Rent

The car rental system allows you to effortlessly add new cars to your inventory, view and check details of available cars, update information on existing cars, and remove cars when needed. This ensures smooth and efficient handling of your car rental operations, providing a seamless experience for you and your customers.

Manage Vehicles For Sale

The car sales system allows for the seamless addition of new vehicles to the inventory, viewing and accessing details of cars available for sale, updating information on current listings, and removing cars as they are sold. This ensures efficient and organized management of your car sales operations, providing a hassle-free experience for your team.

Manage Users

The user management system facilitates the smooth handling of user accounts, including creating new user profiles, accessing and updating user information, and deactivating accounts/deleting accounts when necessary.

Manage Bookings

The booking management system efficiently handles vehicle rental reservations, viewing and updating booking details, and canceling reservations as necessary. This ensures streamlined management of rental bookings, promoting organization and seamless operations for your vehicle rental service.

View Purchases

The purchase management system efficiently handles vehicle rental transactions and allows viewing of purchase details. This ensures streamlined management of rental purchases, promoting organization and seamless operations for your vehicle rental service.
